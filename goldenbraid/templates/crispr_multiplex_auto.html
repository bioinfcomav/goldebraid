Easy CRISPR Cas9 multiplexing

Select the number of guide RNAs you want to include in your construct. Notice that all these guides will be assembled together in the same order you introduce them and in a single polycistron. These polycistronic RNA will be processed in planta producing individual functional guide RNAs (tRNA-based strategy).

See Xie et al. for details on the use of gRNA-tRNA arrays for multiplex genome editing and Vazquez-Vilar et al. to learn more about the assembly of multiplexing constructs with GB.
